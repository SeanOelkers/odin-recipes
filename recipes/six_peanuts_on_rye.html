Six Peanuts on Rye

Ingredients

  • Freshly-shelled peanuts (6)
  • Elmers' Glue (1tsp)
  • Rye bread (2 slices)

Instructions

  1. Lay out your two slices of rye
  2. Slather one rye slice with the Elmers' Glue
  3. Equally place the six peanuts into the spread glue
  4. Cover with second slice of rye
  5. Enjoy!
